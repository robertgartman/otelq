# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Purpose
Describe **why** this product or capability exists and **how success is measured**.
Use it as a **north star** and keep it high-level.

## Problem Statement
(What user or business problem are we solving?)

## Goals
(What outcomes must be achieved?)

## Non-Goals
(Explicitly out of scope.)

## Target Users
(Who this is for.)

## Success Metrics
(How success is evaluated.)

## Constraints
(Business, legal, cost, UX, time.)

## Risks & Open Questions
(Unknowns that may affect scope.)

> Do NOT include implementation details, APIs, or technical solutions.
> Minimize the use of code examples. **Be less prescriptive and more declarative.**
